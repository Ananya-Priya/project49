Intersting facts about Camel

  • When a camel finally does find water, he can drink up to 40 gallons in one go.
  • Camels are very strong and can carry up to 900 pounds for 25 miles a day
  • Camels can travel at up to 40 miles per hour – the same as a racehorse!

Intersting facts about Chameleon

  1. CHAMELEONS VARY WILDLY IN TERMS OF SIZE.
  2. SKIN CRYSTALS ENABLE THEM TO CHANGE COLOR AT WILL.
  3. UNLIKE MANY LIZARDS, CHAMELEONS CAN’T REGROW THEIR TAILS.

Intersting facts about Pigeon

  • THEY SAVED THOUSANDS OF HUMAN LIVES DURING WORLD WARS I AND II.
  • THEY UNDERSTAND SPACE AND TIME.
  • THEY CAN FIND THEIR WAY BACK TO THE NEST FROM 1300 MILES AWAY.

Intersting facts about Teddy

  1. A teddy has been into space! Magellan T Bear boarded Space Shuttle Discovery in 1995 as part of a school project.
  2. The teddy was named after President Theodore Roosevelt, after he refused to shoot a bear during a 1902 hunting trip.
  3. They aren’t just for children – 40% of teddy sales are for adults, and a quarter of us still has our childhood companion.

Intersting facts about Panda

  • Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  • Pandas have carnivorous teeth, but they eat bamboo and fruit.
  • An adult can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!

Intersting facts about Flying-Cicada

  1. Cicadas make noise like toy frogs.
  2. Their enemies are the stuff of nightmares.
  3. They’re underground builders.
